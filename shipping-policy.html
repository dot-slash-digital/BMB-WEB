shipping policy.
Order processing times are separate from the shipping times you see at checkout. All orders are processed within 1 to 5 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.
There could be delays from time to time due to high volume of orders or postal service problems that are outside of our control.
Domestic Shipping
Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
International Shipping
We offer international shipping to all countries available at checkout.
Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout.
Your order may be subject to import duties and taxes (including VAT), which are incurred once a shipment reaches your destination country. be mindful beverages is not responsible for these charges if they are applied and are your responsibility as the customer.
Please note that on rare occasions, customs agents may delay the delivery of some packages at their discretion.
For more information on customs and duties charges, please contact your local customs office.

In-Store Pickup

Refunds, returns, and exchanges
Please review our detailed refund policy here.
We accept returns up to 30 days after delivery, if the item is unused and in its original condition, and we will refund the full order amount minus the shipping costs for the return.
In the event that your order arrives damaged in any way, please email us as soon as possible at info@bemindfulbeverages.com with your order number and a photo of the item’s condition. We address these on a case-by-case basis but will try our best to work towards a satisfactory solution.
If you have any further questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at info@bemindfulbeverages.com.
